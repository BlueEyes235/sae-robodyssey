Problem-Topics

Smart Waste Management Bot

Design and build a multifunctional robot that can assist in efficient waste management in urban environments. The robot should be capable of autonomously performing key tasks related to waste sorting, transportation, and disposal in a smart city setting.

Search And Rescue Bot

Design an autonomous search and rescue bot capable of navigating hazardous envirounment , detecting survivors using acoustic sensors,avoiding obstacles, and providing real time data to the rescue team
